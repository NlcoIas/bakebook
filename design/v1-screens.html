Bulk ferment

Cover the dough and let it rise in a warm spot until roughly doubled. Don't trust the timer — trust the dough.

The poke test: floured finger gently into the side. It should spring back slowly, leaving a faint impression. If it springs back fast, give it more time. If it doesn't spring back at all, you're a touch overproofed.

If the kitchen is below 20°C, this can take 30–45 min longer.

Principles
Two moods, one app. Cream paper for thinking and remembering. Espresso amber for doing.
Mono is for numbers. Grams, percentages, timers, dates, labels. Everything else is the serif.
Tap targets ≥ 44 px. Kitchen hands. Floury fingers.
Hand-drawn rules. SVG paths with a wobble. No straight CSS borders for section dividers.
Photos lead. The journal is a stack of photos with text under them, never the inverse.
No purple. No Inter. Ever.
